Marketing

Why is marketing important?
marketing can be an important tool to connect students with committes or services. Through marketing, institutions, and individuals can provide a legitimized voice to promote objectives.

Other reasons marketing is important include:

  • Helping to educate studdents about work fields.
  • Providing people with new solutions to challenges.
  • Creating or building a target audience.
  • Developing and maintaining members loyalty.
  • Saving time, money and effort by focusing on initiatives that reach the right audience.
  • Creating a more relatable, caring, ethically minded team image.

  • Writing any post that comes down on the team page.Communicating with the public, informing them as much as possible, and responding to them.
